Identifying Parthenium

Parthenium hysterophorus is characterized by deep-lobed leaves and small white flowers. It often causes contact dermatitis in humans and is toxic to livestock.

Read Identification Guide arrow_forward

Safe Removal Protocols

wear protective gloves and masks. Uproot the plant before flowering to prevent pollen spread. Dispose of by composting in a deep pit or burning (with caution).
